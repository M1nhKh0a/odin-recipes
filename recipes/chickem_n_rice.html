Chicken & Rice

Description

Tender, juicy chicken eaten with chicken flavoured rice will be in your comfort dish list. The dish is popular in many South East Asian countries like Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand and Vietnam.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Chicken
  • Ginger
  • Green onion
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Poach the chicken with ginger and green onion.
  2. Using fat from chicken skin to stir fry washed rice with garlic, ginger until golden
  3. Add your chicken broth from earlier and cook in low heat.
  4. Assemble your chicken and rice with soup and sauce (recipe coming soon) and enjoy!
