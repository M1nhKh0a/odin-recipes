Carbonara

Description

The creamy white sauce pasta dish that worth trying. Forget about heavy cream, this traditional Italian recipe will make it uniquely attractive

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Egg
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Guanciale
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Frying Guanciale until golden crispy and fat comes out.
  2. In a bowl, add 3 egg yolks and 1 whole egg, grated Pecorino cheese, Guanciale and its fat, ad pepper. Then, mix it all toghether.
  3. To boil the pasta, add salt when it's boiling and cook the pasta until al dente.
  4. Add the pasta water to thicken the sauce
  5. Finally, mix the pasta with the gorgeous Carbonara sauce and enjoy!
