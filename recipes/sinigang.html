Sinigang

Description

This sweet & sour soup will make you mind-blown and comforting. This is also my favorite way to eat vegetable, since you can add any kinds of greens.

Ingredients

  • Short ribs
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Tamarind concentrate
  • Daikon
  • Carrot
  • Water spinach
  • Taro
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Stir fry the ribs with aromatics like garlic and shallot, season with fish sauce.
  2. Then, saute your tomatoes and onion until soften. Pour some water and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add tamarind concentrate for the soup base, and sugar to balance the sourness of it.
  4. Add hard root vegetable like daikon, carrot, taro to cook first
  5. Simmer in low heat until the ribs tender.
  6. Add other kinds of veggie and continue to cook them
  7. Finally, serve with rice and enjoy!
